About Us
Welcome to Raivat Stones, Grower of CVD lab-grown diamonds, with our headquarters in India, we take pride in being at the forefront of the diamond industry. Founded in 2017 by Mr. Akshat Chaudhary, Raivat Stones benefits from his 8 years of expertise gained through his association with Poojan Diamond. With the blessings of Bharatbhai Chaudhary, a co-founder of Poojan Diamond, Mr. Chaudhary embarks on a new journey in the lab-grown diamond industry.

From sourcing the seed to handcrafting beautiful jewelry ensure meticulous attention to detail every step of the way. Thank you for considering Raivat Stones for your CVD lab-grown diamond needs. We eagerly anticipate the opportunity to serve you and assist you in finding the perfect diamond for your requirements.
How We Became Raivat Stones
Currently, we are manufacturer, wholesaler and supplier of CVD & HPHT Lab-grown diamonds and hold the immense capacity to deliver all sizes of Type IIA, Conflict-Free & Ethically manufactured, Certified & Non-certified CVD & HPHT Lab-grown diamonds, to the B2B Clienteles, Retailers and jewelry Traders globally, by assuring them to supply these genuine diamonds at the best and affordable price with consistency in Quality as well as Quantity.

We offer various varieties of diamonds shapes such as Brilliant Round, Cushion, Heart, Marquise, Pear, Princess, Radiant, Square Radiant, Emerald and Oval. The color ranges from D to J, in the sizes starting from 0.30 to 6.00 Carats. Moreover, We are capable to tie up with clients for customized programs for specific ventures.
