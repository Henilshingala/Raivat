Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2025

At Raivat Stones, we care about your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you browse our website or contact us via WhatsApp or email.

1. What Information We Collect

  • Your Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Messages sent via WhatsApp or Email
  • Any additional info you voluntarily provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to respond to your inquiries, share product details, and coordinate orders through WhatsApp or email. We do not use your data for automated marketing or third-party advertising.

3. How We Collect It

We only collect data when you:

  • Send us a message on WhatsApp
  • Contact us via email
  • Fill out a contact form

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share or sell your information. Only when required (e.g. delivery), your data may be shared with logistics partners with your permission.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use basic cookies or analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand how users interact with the site. This data is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access or correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your personal info
  • Opt out of future communications

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your messages and data shared via email or WhatsApp. No sensitive information is stored on this website.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any changes will appear here with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
